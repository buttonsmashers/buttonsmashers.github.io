Why are you playing?

Video Games are more than just a passion for me. They helped me figure out who I am. Being the hero, solving puzzles, or laughing with friends, games have always brought joy into my life. No matter the challenge, games have always been there to back me up. I want others to feel the same joy. I am huge fan of Speedrunning and the organization "Games Done Quick". When I lost my father, GDQ is what I clung to, and their livestream for charity sparked something inside of me Ill never forget. They were helping save lives like other fathers and familes. I made a decision to join this community to help spread the love in this world. I learned to Speedrun my favorite game, Paper Mario The Thousand Year Door, and since then Ive always hoped to join charity streams in hopes of making a difference in someones life. Whether its raising money, or sparking a passion in someone like me. For me, this is going a step further than just streaming and Youtubing, this is me making a difference in someones life. Maybe someone will watch me livestream for charity and I can spark the same thing inside them to make a difference and spread the love in this world.
